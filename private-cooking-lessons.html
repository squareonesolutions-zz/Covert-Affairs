Oahu Private Cooking Lessons

Cooking in the last decade as become a popular pastime. As more TV programs and cooking magazines come onto the market you may feel that your skills are not as dynamic as you want them to be. Chef Ryan can provide you with private cooking lessons to help guide you though important culinary skill, which you can apply to your everyday life!

  • Appetizer Party: Create 2-3 small bites with friends and then learn how to pair them with wine to enhance the taste & experience of your pallet.

  • Healthy Living: Chef Ryan will teach you how to create healthy meals options to add energy to your life style and help you reach your dietary goals.

  • Sweet Treats: Get a group together and learn how to create some delicious & unique desserts.
  • Romantic Dinner: Impress your loved one by learning to prepare an elegant and romantic dinner for two.
  • BBQ Bash: Learn how to grill like a pro!
